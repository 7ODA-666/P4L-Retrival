About Term Document Matrix

A term-document matrix is a mathematical matrix that describes the frequency of terms that occur in a collection of documents. In this boolean model, we indicate the presence or absence of a term with a 1 or 0.

Preprocessing Importance:

Tokenization, stop-word removal, and lemmatization reduce the vocabulary size and group similar words together, improving retrieval accuracy and efficiency.
