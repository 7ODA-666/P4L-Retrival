What is Information Retrieval?

Information Retrieval (IR) is the science of finding relevant documents or information from large collections based on user queries. It powers search engines like Google, recommendation systems, and knowledge bases.

Core Concept

Finding relevant documents efficiently from massive databases

Real-World Use

Google search, document retrieval, content discovery

Key Operations

Indexing, querying, ranking, and relevance scoring
